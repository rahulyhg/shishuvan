    Welcome to ShishuVihar

    A School with a difference!

    Our educational approach embraces the fact that every learner who comes to our school comes with varied needs, interests and abilities. To meet these and enable students maximize their potential, we believe it is important to encourage the pursuit of academic excellence within a caring environment that promotes each student’s ability and learning style.

    Our Educational Pillars

    International Mindedness

    We celebrate diversity and encourage our students to learn about and respect other people’s cultures and beliefs.

    Principled

    We aim to develop students who at all times act with integrity and honesty.

    Compassion

    Our school culture is built around the theme of empathy and respect

    Responsibility

    We encourage and look forward to our students taking ownership of their own learning.

    Reflective

    Our students are concerned with the process as much as the end product of their learning.

    Innovative

    Our teaching pedagogy and assessment aims to enable students apply their learning in familiar as well as unfamiliar contexts.

    Our Academic Programme

    Early Years Curriculum

    Our early years curriculum comprises the following classes: Nursery – Junior Kindergarten – Senior Kindergarten. The Early Years Program (EYP) consists of the first years of school life (ages 3 to 5). In the EYP, we aim to provide a safe and stimulating environment focusing on discovering and transdisciplinary learning wherein children can develop socially, emotionally, creatively, physically and academically.

    Primary School Curriculum

    The Primary School comprises Grades 1 to 5. In this section, children follow the Cambridge Primary Curriculum covering a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, English, Hindi, Marathi, Science, Humanities, ICT (Computers), Visual Arts, Music and Physical Education.
