Admission FAQs

a. What is the age cut-off for the pre-primary (early years) section?

Nursery: Students should be three years old by 30th September of their Nursery program school year.

Junior Kindergarten - Students should be four years old by 30th September of their Junior kindergarten program school year.

Senior Kindergarten - Students should be five years old by 30th September of their Senior kindergarten program school year.

b. Does the school offer transportation?

Yes, we have a school bus facility. For details on routes, please get in touch with our admission associates.

c. What is the class size in each grade level?

The class size for all our classes is 30.

d. What is the co-curricular program that the school offers?

At ShishuVihar School, our learners will have the opportunity to engage themselves in different forms of art (visual / music / dramatics) and an exclusive STEM Program.

e. What are the indoor and outdoor play facilities at ShishuVihar School?

We have an exclusive indoor play area created specially for our early years and primary section learners. Further, students would have access to sand and water play areas and a central atrium for the PE program.
