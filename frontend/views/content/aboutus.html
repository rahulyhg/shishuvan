History & Legacy

ShishuVihar School has been established by the Shree KVO Sthanakwasi Jain Mahajan Trust, The trust was formed in the year 1873 and since 1917 has been running several schools. In 2017, the trust celebrated its centeneary year and the ShishuVihar School was conceptualized and planned as a result of the same.

Since inception the trust has been responsive to the needs of the community and an international school (ShishuVihar School) came into being as a result of the same.

School Mission and Vision Statements

Our mission is to provide a globally accepted and contextually relevant trans-disciplinary curriculum, to nurture holistic development, inculcate mindfulness and enrich our inquirers to realise their human potential.

Our vision is to nurture lifelong learners for a growth mindset rooted in Indian ethos, in an environment of cultural diversity and mutual respect, developing them as effective change-makers for a sustainable future.
